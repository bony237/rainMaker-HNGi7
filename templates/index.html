RainMaker Technology

Welcome to a world of possibilities.
With our state of the art wind turbine and condensation technology, we have mastered the art of directional wind change and atmospheric water generation. Such directional wind change can influence cloud movement and reduce/stop rainfall while our condensation technology is applied to lower air temperature to it’s dew point, causing water to condense and the result is rainfall.
